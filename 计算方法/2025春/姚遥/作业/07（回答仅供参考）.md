解：取 B=A-6I=\begin{pmatrix}0&2&1 \\\ 2 & -3 & 1 \\\1&1&-5\end{pmatrix}，则转为用反幂法求 B 的最小的特征值 \lambda\_3，即用幂法求 B^{-1}=\begin{pmatrix}0.519 & 0.407 & 0.185\\\0.407&-0.037&0.074\\\0.185&0.074&-0.148\end{pmatrix} 的最大特征值 \lambda'\_1。令 v\_{0}=u\_{0}=\begin{bmatrix} \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{ 3 }} & \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{ 3 }} & \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{ 3 }}\end{bmatrix}^T，采用规范化幂法求得 \dfrac{1}{\lambda\_{3}}=\lambda'\_1\approx \lambda\_1'^{(9)}=0.776，\boldsymbol{x}=u^{(9)}=\begin{bmatrix}1.000&0.523&0.242\end{bmatrix}^T。故 A 的最接近 6 的特征值为 \dfrac{1}{0.776}+6=7.287，特征向量为 \boldsymbol{x}=\begin{bmatrix}1.000&0.523&0.242\end{bmatrix}^T。

解：对于 k=1,确定变换 \boldsymbol{U}\_{1}=\begin{bmatrix}1&0&0\\\0\\\0&&\boldsymbol{R}\_{1}\end{bmatrix}，\boldsymbol{a}\_{21}^{(1)}=\begin{pmatrix}3\\\4\end{pmatrix}

计算 \begin{bmatrix} \boldsymbol{A}\_{12}^{(1)} \\\ \left( \boldsymbol{R}\_1 \boldsymbol{A}\_{22}^{(1)} \right)\end{bmatrix} \boldsymbol{R}\_1=\begin{pmatrix}-5 & 0\\\2.92&0.56\\\0.56&-0.92\end{pmatrix}

> 试用初等反射阵将 A=\begin{pmatrix}1&1&1\\\2&-1&-1\\\2&-4&5\end{pmatrix} 分解为 QR，其中 Q 是正交阵，R 为上三角阵。

解：对于第一列，取 \boldsymbol{a}\_{11}^{(1)}=\begin{pmatrix}1 \\\ 2 \\\ 2\end{pmatrix}，\sigma\_{1}=\lVert \boldsymbol{a}\_{11}^{(1)} \rVert=3，\boldsymbol{u}\_1= \boldsymbol{a}\_{11}^{(1)} + \sigma\_{1} \boldsymbol{e}\_1=\begin{pmatrix}4\\\2\\\2\end{pmatrix}，\rho\_{1}=\sigma\_{1}(\sigma\_{1}+a\_{11})=12，\boldsymbol{H}\_1= \boldsymbol{I}- \rho\_1^{-1} \boldsymbol{u}\_1 \boldsymbol{u}\_1^T=\begin{pmatrix}-0.333&-0.667&-0.667\\\\-0.667&0.667&-0.333\\\\-0.667&-0.333&0.667\end{pmatrix}
\boldsymbol{A}\_{2}=\boldsymbol{H}\_1 \boldsymbol{A}\_1=\begin{pmatrix}-3 & 3 & -3\\\0 & 0 & -3 \\\0 & -3 & 3\end{pmatrix}，取 \boldsymbol{H}\_2=\begin{pmatrix}1 & 0 & 0 \\\0\\\0 & & \boldsymbol{R}\_2\end{pmatrix}
对于第二列，取 \boldsymbol{a}\_{11}^{(2)}=\begin{pmatrix}0 \\\\-3\end{pmatrix}，\sigma\_{2}=\lVert \boldsymbol{a}\_{11}^{(2)} \rVert=3，\boldsymbol{u}\_2= \boldsymbol{a}\_{11}^{(2)}+\sigma\_2 \boldsymbol{e}\_2=\begin{pmatrix}3\\\\-3\end{pmatrix}，\rho\_{2}=\sigma\_{2}(\sigma\_{2}+a\_{22})=9，\boldsymbol{R}\_2= \boldsymbol{I}-\rho\_2^{-1} \boldsymbol{u}\_2 \boldsymbol{u}\_2^T=\begin{pmatrix}0 & 1 \\\ 1 & 0\end{pmatrix}
\boldsymbol{A}\_3=\boldsymbol{H}\_2 \boldsymbol{A}\_2=\begin{pmatrix}-3 & 3& -3 \\\0 & -3 & 3 \\\0&0&-3\end{pmatrix}
因此，\boldsymbol{A}= \boldsymbol{H}\_2 \boldsymbol{H}\_1 \boldsymbol{A}\_3，故 \boldsymbol{Q}= \boldsymbol{H}\_2 \boldsymbol{H}\_1= \begin{pmatrix}-0.333&-0.667&-0.667\\\\-0.667&-0.333&0.667\\\\-0.667&0.667&-0.333\end{pmatrix}，\boldsymbol{R}=\boldsymbol{A}\_3=\begin{pmatrix}-3&3&-3\\\0&-3&3\\\0&0&-3\end{pmatrix}
